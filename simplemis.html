Flame Brooch

By Slycotton

Wondrous Item, Attuned, Rare

This small, scarlet-colored, translucent crystal has swirling runes carved in it, and can be worn to protect its bearer from fire.

While attuned to this brooch, if you are dealt Fire damage, you are instead dealt Force damage and the damage taken is halved (rounded down). After you are protected once, this item will not activate again until 2 turns have passed, or you have exited combat.

Evoker’s Horn

By Slycotton

Wondrous Item, Rare

This worn brass horn is decorated with dull silver spikes arranged in ring formations, and has a pipe to blow it as an instrument.

As an action, you can blow the horn to deal 2d8 Thunder damage to every creature within a 30-foot cube radius around you, including yourself.

After the horn is used in this way, blowing it will make it act as a normal horn until the next dawn.

Cube of Colding

By Slycotton

Wondrous Item, Common

This tiny, glowing cyan cube is cold to the touch and covered in frost. It is small enough to fit in a human’s palm.

While the cube is on your person, you automatically succeed on any saving throw against environmental hot temperatures (E.G. A desert).

The cube can be thrown at a target as an attack action. Roll to hit the target like you would any ranged attack (range 20/60). If the cube hits, it explodes in a small icy spark, and deals 2d4 Cold damage.

These cubes are a standard commodity purchasable at most large and established settlements.

Vampiric Dagger

By Slycotton

Wondrous Item, Dagger. Uncommon

This dagger has a striking red hilt, and is molded into sharp patterns. Its blade is split into two with a small gap inbetween.

When you make a successful melee attack using this dagger, you gain +1d4 Hit Points. This effect only occurs on the first attack of your turn, and on targets other than Constructs or Undead.

Conch of Unbreaking

By Slycotton

Wondrous Item, Uncommon

This pink conch shell has striking blue dots and patterns painted on it.

This shell cannot be destroyed or have its base form modified by any means.
